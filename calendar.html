About Us


Mommy Yoona Pet Supplies and Accessories Shop was founded on May 15, 2023 at 1207 B. Quiricada Street Tondo, Manila through the efforts of Mr. Rodelio G. Castro and his family. Noticing the increase in demand for animal-related products and care, Mr. Castro decided to open a pet supplies and accessories shop in order to cater to the needs of animal owners as he already has a hands-on experience and knowledge in animal care due to his and his family’s love for animals. Furthermore, the location of Mommy Yoona Pet Supplies and Accessories Shop provides an edge for the business as there are only a few animal-pet related businesses around the area.
